About Kenyan Coffee

Kenya produces some of the world's finest Arabica coffee, grown in the rich volcanic soils of the highlands at altitudes between 1,400 and 2,000 meters above sea level.

Kenyan coffee is renowned for its bright acidity, full body, and distinctive wine-like flavor profile. Small-scale farmers cultivate most of Kenya's coffee through cooperatives, ensuring quality control and fair trade practices. The unique climate, combined with meticulous wet processing methods, produces beans that consistently rank among the world's best specialty coffees.
